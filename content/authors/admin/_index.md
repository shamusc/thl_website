Shamus Cooley, PhD, is a bioinformatics-trained scientist and engineering leader working at the intersection of biological data, machine intelligence, and scientific software. He is the founder of Turtle House Labs, an independent research initiative focused on microbiome-informed agricultural biocontrol discovery.

His work spans genomics, microbiome analysis, single-cell RNA-seq, scientific knowledge graphs, and AI-assisted scientific workflows. He is interested in making computational biology more useful for practical biological and agricultural problems.
